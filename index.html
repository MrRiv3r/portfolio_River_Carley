I wanted to show the idea of how creative ideas are formed in three different styles. the left most image is about the cristilisation of an idea and done in a realsistic stye in corel painter. the senter most image is about how the mind stretches to accomidate new ideas and done in a surearealistic style and in photo shop. the right mnost image is about how the mind travels on a current and i represented the mind as an abstract leaf on the wind or water and done with more traditional mediams of marker and pen. the right most is my favorite of the three becuase it looks like a lot of different things when rotated
